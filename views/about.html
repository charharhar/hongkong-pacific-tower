about the
building

Located in the fast developing Kowloon Bay Business Area, the heart of CBD², Hong Kong Pacific Tower is a Grade-A, 24-storey office building with approximately 339,000 ft² of premium retail and office space on offer. The high ceilings and elegant design of the lobby, with beautiful Sky Garden on the 2nd floor offer luxurious open spaces for tenants and retail customers to enjoy.

connectivity &
accessibility

Heart of City with Intensive
Transport Connection

Right in the heart of Kowloon East, Hong Kong Pacific Tower is easily accessible by car or public transportation. When traveling by public transportation, a planned 550 meter long covered walkway will connect Hong Kong Pacific Tower directly to Telford Plaza, for smooth access to a public buses or the MTR at Kowloon Bay Station.

The integration of both office and retail space in Hong Kong Pacific Tower creates a mutually beneficial relationship between the two kinds of tenant. The retail space will provide convenient and high-end options for shopping, meeting spaces and food and beverage before, during and after work hours to sophisticated consumers working in the building.

Hong Kong Pacific Investments Limited (HKPI)

Hong Kong Pacific Investments Limited (HKPI) has over 40 years of experience investing in property in Hong Kong. With an investment portfolio containing approximately 2 million ft² of office, retail, food and beverage, HKPI has a vast amount of experience with large-scale and high-end developments.
